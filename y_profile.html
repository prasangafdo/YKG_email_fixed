Yukthi's Profile

  1. Home
  2. Yukthi's Profile


Mr. Yukthi K. Gunasekera is a communications professional with over 30 years of experience in public speaking. He counts a combined total of 14 years of management experience as Channels Manager at IBM Sri Lanka and as Head of Corporate Communications at John Keells Holdings PLC. Yukthi is a US-educated lawyer who also holds a post-graduate diploma in marketing from the Chartered Institute of Marketing, UK. He is currently a corporate trainer, coach, MC, and TV host and runs his own firm YKG Associates – which teaches communication skills to employees ranging from junior executives to leaders of organisations. He specialises in CEO Communication – how leaders of organisations can use effective communication tools to better achieve organisational goals. Yukthi firmly believes that training in communication skills enables people to lead more rewarding lives.
